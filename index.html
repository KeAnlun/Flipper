The objective of the game is to make each panel flip from its 'X' side to its 'O' side. Clicking on a panel will flip the panel and one other panel that it is linked to.

Enter a difficulty - Try the game with a different number of panels

Retry - The board resets to the starting state, but the links stay the same

Reset - The board resets and new links are generated
